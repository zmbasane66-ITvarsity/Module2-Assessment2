MO ELECTRONICS

MO ELECTRONICS is one of SA's leading online electrical retailers. MO ELECTRONICS has since moved into mainstream electricals, offering everything from TVs, laptops, musical instrument and digital cameras, yet it remains a leading go-to site for computer components. The company's distribution facility was purpose built in 2011 at its headquarters in Port Elizabeth, Eastern Cape from where over 1,000 weekly orders are delivered to customers throughout Southern Africa. At MO electronics, our business philosophy is simple: give customers and businesses what they want, when they want it. We never stop improving our world class operation by putting our customers - and their needs - first.

We offer our customers over 4,000 quality products, and this list is growing every day. MO Electronic customers know they're getting the best prices and exclusive offers on a huge range of electricals. You'll find computer technology products like laptops, desktops, printers, software, storage and more. Our audio and visual entertainment categories give you a huge choice ranging from Smart TVs, digital cameras, musical instrument, gaming and home electronics to Experience Day vouchers and gadgets. Our great relationships with all the major vendors mean our customers will always find special deals on great products from the biggest names in the industry. And to help our customers choose the right products, we make it easy to find availability, pricing, reviews and ratings.
