ON SCREEN: Friday @ 19:00


FILM STARS: Jessica Chastain, John Malkovich, Common, Geena Davis, Collin Farrell, Ioan Gruffudd, and Joan Chen.

Ava was released in Hungary on July 2, 2020, and in the United States through DirecTV Cinema on August 27, 2020, followed by a limited theatrical and video on demand release on September 25, 2020, by Vertical Entertainment. Despite Chastain's performance receiving praise, the film received negative reviews.Ava is a deadly assassin who works for a black ops organization, traveling the globe specializing in high profile hits. When a job goes dangerously wrong she is forced to fight for her own survival.
