KING KONG

13 VL

ON SCREEN:Saturday @ 14:30


ACTORS:Andy Serkis, Naomi Watts, Jack Black, and Andrien Brody.

King Kong is a 2005 American monster film co-written, it follows the story of an ambitious filmmaker who coerces his cast and hired ship crew to travel to the mysterious Skull Island. There, they encounter prehistoric creatures living on the island as well as a legendary giant gorilla known as Kong, whom they capture and take to New York City. Filming for King Kong took place in New Zealand from September 2004 to March 2005.
